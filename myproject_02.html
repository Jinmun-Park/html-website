Python Time-Series Collection

  • You can use the package to run time-series models including LSTM, AutoTS, Prophet and RandomForest.

  • The critical point making this package works is transforming your raw data to fit into the list of models. Therefore, i am trying to use many different types of data to create independent preprocessing function.

    • Github Repository

    Lets make the fastest class function for us to find the models at once !

    We are often facing in a situation that we do not know what models we have to apply in our time series data. I had many repeating cases that i am testing out same models and they are mostly conventional modelling techniques. For example, ARIMA and Random forest always work fairly well and most of my ensemble outputs consist of these models. This gives me an idea to put all time series models into a signle function. This will eventually save my time as it will show me what model would be the best fit and having better accuracy.
